Subject Lines · 3 Variants
Three angles. Rationale for each. Pick the one that fits your list.
A — Curiosity gap:
"the hire every $8K founder gets wrong"
States the audience + stakes, hides the answer. Opens the loop.

B — Direct value promise:
"how to make your first hire without burning runway"
Plain outcome. Works on value-seekers who scan subject lines fast.

C — Specificity + number:
"3 signals you're ready to hire (and 1 that means wait)"
Number + asymmetry creates tension. Strong for analytically-minded lists.
All under 60 characters. All lowercase. No spam triggers. Preheader copy included for each.
