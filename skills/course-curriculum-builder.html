Quiz Bank · Module 2
Scenario-based questions, not recall MCQs.
Q2.3 — Scenario · Bloom's: Apply

A client sells project management software to startup CTOs. Their current homepage headline reads: "Powerful Tools for Modern Teams." You've been asked to rewrite it.

Which rewrite best applies the one-reader principle?

A) "The Best Project Management Software Available"
B) "Everything Your Team Needs in One Place"
C) "CTOs at Series A startups use [Product] to ship without the spreadsheet chaos"
D) "Trusted by 10,000+ companies worldwide"

Correct: C · Explanation: C names the exact reader, the exact context, and the exact problem...
Every question includes an explanation of why wrong answers are wrong — not just a correct answer reveal.
