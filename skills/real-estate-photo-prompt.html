Platform Notes · MLS + Social
Where each image goes and how to crop it.
Exterior:
• MLS hero shot — use as first listing image
• Crop 16:9 → Zillow / Realtor.com thumbnail
• Square crop → Instagram save-bait post
Interior:
• MLS gallery positions 2–5 after hero
• Portrait crop → Reels thumbnail / carousel
• Vignette crop (one styled corner) → social
⚠️ Label "virtually enhanced" per MLS / local law
California AB 723 (eff. Jan 1 2026) requires disclosure + original unaltered image on file. Always verify your local MLS rules.
