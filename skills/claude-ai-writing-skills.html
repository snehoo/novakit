What are Claude AI writing skills?

NovaKit writing skills are specialist files you upload to Claude once. After that, whenever you open them, Claude runs live research on what's working in that format right now — then asks you three calibrating questions before writing anything. The result is output calibrated to your voice, your audience, and current format conventions — not a generic template.

Generic Claude is powerful but writes for everyone. A NovaKit writing skill adds the research, the structure, and the quality gate it doesn't have by default. The same intelligence with a specialist framework on top.

All writing skills work with a free Claude account. Upload once, run whenever you need output. If you write across multiple formats, the Creative Bundle gets you six writing skills at a 52% saving.
