Scene 1 · Hero Shot
Product visible within 3 seconds. Scroll stopped.
Platform: TikTok / Reels · 9:16 · 5s

Close-up product hero reveal — matte black skincare serum bottle centred on frosted glass surface. Camera pulls back slowly while the bottle rotates 15°. Soft diffused front light with cool rim highlight. Background: deep slate gradient, no texture. Clean studio isolation. Negative: avoid blurry motion, avoid oversaturated colour, avoid camera shake. Aspect ratio 9:16.
Hook-first composition. Product identity locked via reference image. Platform-safe crop with zero safe-zone violation.
Scene 2 · Reveal
Texture and finish shown up close.
Platform: Meta Feed · 1:1 · 6s

Extreme macro — serum dropper tip with single drop forming and falling in slow motion. Warm side light catches liquid viscosity. Background: soft cream bokeh. Camera: static with micro-drift on subject. Audio reference: quiet, ambient — no music bed. Negative: avoid harsh specular flare, avoid static drop (motion required), avoid full white background. Aspect ratio 1:1.
Texture sells premium. Macro motion in a static frame adds perceived production value without complexity.
Scene 3 · Lifestyle
Real environment. Product in use.
Platform: TikTok / Reels · 9:16 · 8s

Medium shot — woman's hand applying serum to face in front of a bright bathroom mirror, natural morning light streaming in from left. Authentic feel, slight handheld camera movement. Warm skin tones, no heavy colour grade. Product label briefly visible during application. Background: soft bathroom context, not clinical. Negative: avoid studio feel, avoid over-lit skin, avoid posed gesture. Aspect ratio 9:16.
UGC-authentic motion outperforms polished studio in DTC feed environments. Handheld cue signals realness.
Scene 4 · Detail / Texture
Packaging quality shown without a word.
Platform: YouTube Shorts · 16:9 · 6s

Slow orbit around product — 360° pan at product height. Studio dark background with specular edge lighting, controlled reflection on glass bottle. Label crisp and legible at all angles. Camera: smooth continuous orbit, no easing stutter. Negative: avoid shadow obscuring label, avoid motion blur on text, avoid busy background, avoid lens flare on glass. Aspect ratio 16:9.
Orbit shot is the standard premium reveal for YouTube pre-roll and shopping-intent viewers. Lighting spec prevents glass flare failures.
