Voice in one sentence:

Archetype: The Sage
Confident, educational, direct. Earns trust through clarity, not credentials. Never name-drops, never hedges, never over-explains.

Voice Pillars · 3 Defined
Each pillar is named, defined, and proved with a before/after pair.
PILLAR 1 — Direct
Says the thing. No preamble, no hedge, no passive voice.
✓ In voice: "Your data is exposed. Here's how to close it."
✗ Out of voice: "Many organisations may find themselves in a situation where data security could be a concern."

PILLAR 2 — Warm Without Trying
Human because it treats the reader as intelligent — not because it uses exclamation points.
✓ "We won't pretend this is easy. Here's what to do first."
All 3–4 pillars delivered with this level of specificity — each includes: name, meaning, examples, what-it-doesn't-mean, and the before/after pair.
