CEO Note · Momentum Arc
Opens with the headline fact. Sets tone in 3 sentences.
Subject: Lumio — April Update: MRR hit $42k, churn cut in half

April was our best month on record. We crossed $42k MRR — up 18% from March — and the onboarding changes we shipped in Week 2 drove churn down from 4.1% to 1.9%. We have 11 months of runway at current burn, and one specific ask at the bottom of this email that could meaningfully accelerate our Q3 pipeline.
3–4 sentences. Headline metric first. Runway stated. Ask signposted. Human tone — not a memo.

Lowlights + Ask
Lowlights are mandatory. The Ask is specific and actionable.
✗ Lowlights
• Our Head of Growth resigned in Week 3 to join a funded competitor. We had no succession plan in place — that's on us. We've opened a search and have two strong referrals from the team already; we expect to be interviewing by May 15.

The Ask
If you know a VP of Sales at a Series A logistics SaaS (50–300 person ops team), we'd value an introduction — this is our tightest ICP right now and a warm intro converts 4× better than cold outreach for us.
Lowlights: what happened → why → what you're doing. The Ask: named role, named vertical, specific reason.
