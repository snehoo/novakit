Opening hook (Airbnb · 72 words)

Step out onto the rooftop terrace as the city wakes up — espresso in hand, the Duomo five minutes away and entirely unhurried. This 1920s-era apartment has been stripped back and restored: original tilework, floor-to-ceiling windows, and a kitchen stocked for people who actually cook.

Block C · Host Story
The trust builder — beats chains on personality.
About Your Host — Marco & Silvia · 148 words

We're a ceramics teacher and a structural engineer who spent three years restoring this flat above the old hat-maker's workshop — tiles sourced from the original supplier, walls painted the exact shade we found under four layers.

We host because the neighbourhood deserves to be discovered slowly, and most guides miss it entirely. The welcome folder has our actual recommendations — not the tourist circuit.

You'll find a bottle of local olive oil in the kitchen and a handwritten note with the week's market days.

We respond within the hour, every day.
Specific personal detail + genuine reason for hosting + one concrete welcome gesture. Replaces "we love meeting guests from around the world."
Block D · Platform Notes
Adaptation brief for every platform you list on.
VRBO adaptation
Add sleeping-arrangement detail by bedroom. Extend to 620 words. Highlight proximity to family landmarks and nearest supermarket.

Booking.com adaptation
Shorten to 380 words. Front-load amenity keywords: fast WiFi, dedicated workspace, hot tub, air conditioning, EV charger. Use structured short paragraphs — Booking's algorithm indexes explicit amenity mentions.

Direct website
SEO focus: "apartment [city centre] + short stay + [neighbourhood name]". CTA: "Check Dates" not "Request to Book."
2026 best practice: list on both Airbnb and VRBO. Airbnb holds 55–60% of US STR volume; VRBO 20–25%. One master copy, platform-adapted.
