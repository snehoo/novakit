Beat 4 · Pattern Interrupt — Every 60–90s
Visual, audio, or content shift — mandatory in every long-form script.
BEAT 4 — PATTERN INTERRUPT — 3:20–3:35

SPOKEN:
Here's the stat that made me rethink everything.

📊 GRAPHIC: Full-screen stat card — "65% of viewers who pass 15 seconds watch past 3 minutes. Without a hook: under 45%." — white text on dark background, 3 seconds
🎵 AUDIO NOTE: Music drops to silence for 2 seconds as the graphic appears. Resume at lower volume.
⚡ DELIVERY NOTE: Stop talking as the stat appears. Let the viewer read it. Silence is the interrupt.
Pattern interrupts are flagged at the exact beat — not left for the editor to place. Visual change + audio shift together produce the strongest retention signal.
