Post Brief

BRIEF #3 — Instagram Carousel | Pillar: Education

Week: 2 · Phase: 1
Goal: Save-worthy educational post to build authority
Hook: "Your cleanser has 4 ingredients it doesn't need to tell you about."
Angle: Each slide = one ingredient + why it's in there (cost, not benefit)
Caption: Conversational, not clinical. End with: "Save this for your next restock."
CTA: "Save this" — primary; "Tag someone who checks labels" — secondary
Repurpose: 3 x Stories (one slide each) + email teaser paragraph
Every brief names: platform, format, pillar, goal, hook, CTA, visual direction, and repurpose path. No brief is just a topic.
