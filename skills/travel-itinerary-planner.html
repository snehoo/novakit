DAY 2 — THE FISHERMEN'S QUARTER AND ITS RESTAURANTS

Morning: Mercado de Campo de Ourique (Tue–Sun, opens 10am). Not the tourist market — this one serves neighbourhood residents. Go for the petiscos counter on the upper level. Arrive before 11am.

Lunch: Tasca do Chico, Rua do Diário de Notícias 39. Order the bacalhau à brás. Book 2 days ahead — 12 covers only. $$

Afternoon: Miradouro da Graça — 20-min walk uphill via Alfama's back streets. The view faces away from the castle, which is why the photos are better.
