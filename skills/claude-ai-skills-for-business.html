Why business documents need audience calibration

A pitch deck for a pre-seed investor is not the same document as one for a Series A investor. An NDA for a freelance contractor is not the same as one for a technology partnership. Generic Claude doesn't know the difference — it produces the same document for every context.

NovaKit business skills ask three calibrating questions about the audience, the stage, and the stakes before writing anything. The Pitch Deck Narrative skill knows what a pre-seed investor needs versus a Series A investor. The Business Plan skill knows what a bank evaluates versus what a co-founder evaluates. The output is built for the person reading it — not the person requesting it.
