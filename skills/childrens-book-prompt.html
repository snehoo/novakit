Colour palette guide
Scene-by-scene palette notes for the illustrator.
Opening spreads (pages 5–10): Warm morning light — cream, ochre, soft terracotta. Milo's world feels safe and small.

Conflict spreads (pages 11–20): Cooler, slightly desaturated. Sage shifts to grey-green. Milo's uncertainty visible in the palette.

Resolution spreads (pages 21–28): Palette warms again — ochre returns, fuller saturation. The emotional shift is colour-led.
Colour palette shifts are mapped to the story's emotional arc — warmth for safety, cool desaturation for tension, warmth restored at resolution.
