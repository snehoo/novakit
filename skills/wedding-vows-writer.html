Delivery notes
Practical guidance for the day itself.
Before the ceremony:
Read aloud at least five times — emotion changes pacing completely. Time yourself speaking, not reading silently. Print in large font, double-spaced.

On the day:
Speak to your partner, not to the paper. If your voice breaks — pause, breathe, smile at them, continue. The room is with you, not judging you.

Speak more slowly than feels right. Nerves speed everyone up.
