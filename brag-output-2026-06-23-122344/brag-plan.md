# NovaKit Brag Video Plan
**Format:** 9:16 (Vertical)  
**Duration:** 18 seconds  
**Tone:** Cinematic-Epic + Kid-Simple (epic trailer music underscoring a deliberately simple metaphor — the mismatch is the charm)  
**Target:** Explainer for 5-year-olds (simple metaphors, visual impact, pure joy)

## Planning Rubric

### 1. **What does this product do?**
NovaKit is a marketplace of "Claude Skills"—superpowers for Claude AI that research live trends before they write, so your content is never stale. Buy individual skills ($5–$19) or bundles ($15–$69) to unlock prompts for LinkedIn posts, pitch decks, cold emails, podcasts, and 60+ more tasks.

### 2. **Who is the buyer?**
Content creators, entrepreneurs, marketers, students, anyone who writes at work and wants AI-powered shortcuts that actually know what's happening *right now*.

### 3. **What's the core tension/problem?**
Generic AI tools spit out cookie-cutter output. ChatGPT forgot what happened yesterday. Your social media advice is stale. Your competitor research is 3 months old.

### 4. **What's the boldest claim in the product?**
"Skills that research live trends before every output." Every time you use a NovaKit skill, it runs real research *first*, then writes—so you get fresh, relevant, non-generic results.

### 5. **What's the visual signature?**
- Dark theme interface with a warm coral accent (#DE7356)
- Skill cards with emoji icons (📋, ✉️, 🎨, 💼, etc.)
- Live research badges ("Trending now", green dot pulse)
- Stacked skill bundles, pricing tiers

### 6. **What's the hook?**
"Your AI forgot what happened yesterday. NovaKit didn't." — Blunt, funny, relatable.

### 7. **What story can we tell?**
A 5-year-old's metaphor: "Imagine if your robot friend read the news before helping you. NovaKit is that friend."

### 8. **What's the outcome/call-to-action?**
Visit novakit.tech, browse 63 skills, pick one, buy it, use it, get results that aren't stale.

### 9. **What scenes are essential?**
- Hook: Stale content vs. fresh content (visual contrast)
- Reveal: NovaKit skills grid, flashing emojis
- Highlight 1: "Research runs first" (live data, research badge)
- Highlight 2: Bundle magic (6 skills in one, value cascade)
- Highlight 3: Pricing (from $5 to $69)
- Punchline: "Never write stale again" + CTA

## Style Notes

- **Fast cuts, not frenetic:** Sleep-cut tempo means snappy transitions every 1–2 seconds, but each shot is *readable*. Hold on text long enough for viewers to read (0.8–1.2s per short phrase).
- **Dark theme enforces pop:** The dark interface makes the coral accent (#DE7356) and green research badges *pop*. Lean into high contrast.
- **Emoji as character:** Each skill emoji is a little personality. They rotate, pulse, or scale slightly to feel alive.
- **No generic SaaS language:** Avoid "streamline," "maximize," "synergy." Use actual product copy: "Live research," "Fresh trends," "Never stale."
- **5-year-old logic:** "Robot forgot the news, NovaKit reads it first." Simple metaphor, visual proof.
- **Epic music, dumb-simple script:** Lean into the contrast on purpose — trailer-grade strings/synths under text like "robot friend" and "reads the news first." Don't let the copy try to be epic too; that kills the joke.
