Variant 1 · Direct
Leads with the pain. No preamble.
Subject: "carrier invoice errors eating margin"

Most logistics ops teams we talk to are spending 8–12 hours a week manually reconciling carrier invoices — errors they catch too late to dispute.

We cut that to under 90 minutes for ops teams at [similar company]. No TMS replacement — works alongside what you have.

Worth a 15-min call this week?
50–125 words. Problem-aware opener. Objection pre-empted naturally. One low-friction CTA.
Variant 2 · Insight-led
Opens with an observation, not a pitch.
Subject: "why logistics ops teams keep the same problem"

Most carrier invoice errors aren't a process problem — they're a data visibility problem. The dispute window closes before anyone knows the error exists.

We built a fix that flags errors in real time, before the invoice is approved. [Company] went from 23% dispute rate to under 4% in 60 days.

Curious whether you're seeing the same pattern?
C-suite / VP-level angle. Insight before solution. Invites a perspective exchange, not a demo.
Variant 3 · Social Proof
Opens with a result from a similar company.
Subject: "how [logistics co] recovered $340k in 90 days"

A regional 3PL with your carrier mix was losing $340K a year to disputed invoices they couldn't catch in time. We automated the reconciliation — they recovered it in a quarter.

No rip-and-replace. Works alongside your existing TMS.

Worth a quick chat?
Risk-averse buyers and competitive markets. Specific dollar amount beats "a company like yours."
