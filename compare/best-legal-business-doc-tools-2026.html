Common questions

Can AI write a legally binding NDA?
AI can draft an NDA that covers standard confidentiality terms, exclusions, duration, and jurisdiction. Whether it is legally binding depends on correct execution (signatures, consideration, jurisdiction compliance). The NovaKit NDA skill produces a professional draft and recommends lawyer review before signing anything with significant consequences.
Is NovaKit's legal bundle suitable for Indian freelancers?
Yes. The skills include Indian jurisdiction options — IT Act 2000, Indian Contract Act, and relevant data protection framing. The ToS and Privacy Policy skill includes notes on India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 requirements.
Does the visa cover letter skill work for all visa types?
The Visa Application Cover Letter skill is calibrated for common visa categories — tourist, business, student, work permit, and skilled worker visas — across major destinations (UK, US, EU, Canada, Australia). You specify the visa type and destination country in the intake.
