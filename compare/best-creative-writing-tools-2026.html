Common questions

What is the best AI tool for writing short stories in 2026?
Sudowrite is the strongest purpose-built fiction tool for long-form. For structured short fiction frameworks — genre, character, conflict, arc — NovaKit's Short Story Prompt skill produces a detailed story framework you write from, which tends to produce more distinctly authored work than pure AI generation.
Can AI write a good screenplay?
AI can produce a scene, dialogue, and structure scaffold. The NovaKit Screenplay Script skill generates a full scene-by-scene breakdown with character beats and dialogue — you write the final screenplay from that scaffold. Full AI-generated screenplays tend to feel formulaic; the scaffold approach preserves the writer's voice.
Does NovaKit write wedding speeches and vows?
Yes. The Wedding Vows Writer skill is in the Creative Bundle — it captures the couple's story, tone (emotional, humorous, or mixed), and key moments before writing. The Event Speech Writer skill covers toasts, best man/maid of honour speeches, and wedding ceremony scripts.
